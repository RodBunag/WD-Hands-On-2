Eevee

Its ability to evolve into many forms allows it to adapt smoothly and perfectly to any environment. Having gotten even friendlier and more innocent, Eevee tries to play with anyone around, only to end up crushing them with its immense body. Gigantamax energy upped the fluffiness of the fur around Eevee's neck.
